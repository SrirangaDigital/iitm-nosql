The women of course, wore the pants in the house
while the men's uniform was a pre-stressed blouse
Little boys worked hard all day

People were born at the age of ninety
and then grew younger in steps of twenty
They started growing older at the age of ten
and have been doing so ever since then.

Having listened to this strange description
I thanked my stars this guy was fiction
