The Sinner
Just this time,
and never again-

Not before the
vow is broken:
Oh, let it, let it,-

the pleasure of pain,
Yes-Oh, never,
but in vain.
I here confess,
at your clemency.
Sins not my heart,
knowingly.
I strive to reach,
the endless shore.
Alas, the tide impedes,
once more.
